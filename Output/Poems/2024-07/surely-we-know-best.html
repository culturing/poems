Surely We Know Best

27 July 2024

It is a miracle each time, but,
none the wiser, we plod on,
transforming Nature's bounty,
making hers our own. What is life?
Just the animating principle, just
the motive force, the cause of growth,
just everything that matters? But
no matter, surely we know best.
Let's kill it all.
