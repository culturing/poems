Anatomy of Shadows

31 January 2026

https://poems.culturing.net/2026/01/anatomy-of-shadows/

Are you on the hunt, and am I the prey?
Do you like to play with your food?
Well then I must say this will not last long.
I have been both predator and prey before,
and I find both boring. It's better to love
and be loved or be left alone. But the shadow
of love lives in each of us, let's not deny it.
I think it's been found near the cervix.
