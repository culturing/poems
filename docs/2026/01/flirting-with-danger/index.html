Flirting With Danger

08 January 2026

https://poems.culturing.net/2026/01/flirting-with-danger/

You make it hard
to do the right thing, and I like that,
flirting with danger, but only to make love sure.
Are you sure? Would you give me that power?
I'd welcome it, but I must know what you want.

You may have the guest bed.
There is room in my bed as well.
Which do you prefer?
