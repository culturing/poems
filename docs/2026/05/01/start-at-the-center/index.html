Start at the Center

01 May 2026

https://poems.culturing.net/2026/05/01/start-at-the-center/

Who shall I be now, after journeying
into myself and back again, aware
of the cosmic vibrations in my own heart?
I will start at the center, and find there
connections with others, a place to believe
and a place to dream, but a place
closed to outsiders and wolves.
