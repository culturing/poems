Postscript to Venus in Fur

20 April 2026

https://poems.culturing.net/2026/04/20/postscript-to-venus-in-fur/

Vanda has her way with him,
then leaves him dangling in pain, half-crucified.
But then comes the resurrection,
Thomas enlivened with newfound strength,
a hidden power allowing him to understand women,
to treat them like women with kindness and respect. He cancels the play
and finds another more suited to his new personality.
