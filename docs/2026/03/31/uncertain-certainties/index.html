Uncertain Certainties

31 March 2026

https://poems.culturing.net/2026/03/31/uncertain-certainties/

The end is a new beginning, like always
and whatever comes will be new, but familiar,
an echo with small modulations, the same old song
with new harmonies, counterpoint answering theme
with the age-old questions, the uncertain certainties,
raining from heaven or bubbling up from the earth.
