Only Together

30 June 2026

https://poems.culturing.net/2026/06/30/only-together/

Anima and Animus rise through time
to their fated meeting, collide and again
decide to go on together, to live as one whole,
against chaos, against the formless dance
of isolation and painful solitude. Only together
can either make sense. They will find their way
to each other. They have no choice.
