Being

04 January 2019

https://poems.culturing.net/2019/01/being/

For Heidegger

It is all jumbled together,
yin and yang,

at once the thing
and the corruption of the thing,

and we all know the dirty bath may need to go,
babe be damned,

But then,
how can we hope?

How does one cope?
