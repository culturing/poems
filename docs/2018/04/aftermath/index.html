Aftermath

21 April 2018

https://poems.culturing.net/2018/04/aftermath/

I have heard the wild
    ramblings,
felt betrayed by man
    and steel,
and I cannot
    keep on
good clothes —

    but madly naked
run through city streets,
    cry “Kung Fu Tze!
where are you?”
