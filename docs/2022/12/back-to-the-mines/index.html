Back to the Mines

15 December 2022

I think sometimes we shall never escape
from the general, all-around clusterfuck
(pardon the language, it applies) of humanity
always and everywhere, in general confused
about what it all means, about what meaning means,
or why meaning is so indispensable.

Oh well, let's back to the mines
for another long spell of formation through labor.
