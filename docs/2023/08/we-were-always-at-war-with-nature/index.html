17 August 2023

https://poems.culturing.net/2023/08/we-were-always-at-war-with-nature/

We were always at war with nature
or in love with it, at all times not
seeing clearly nor penetrating deeper than
whatever suited our momentary fancy,
but I for one saw her differently,
not afraid to delve into her secrets
even if it meant losing myself.
