Open Hearts

18 December 2025

https://poems.culturing.net/2025/12/open-hearts/

Who can say, as day
bleeds into day, whether love
will arrive and arrive to stay?
It's okay and it's not at all okay.
But hey, this is life with open hearts.
It's what sets us apart.
