Fire and Earth
15 April 2026

My fire burns for the image of love,
for the vision of potentials fulfilled in a sacred dance,
but I will not dance with blasphemers.
The gods of love are too real.

---

But if I step down from the temple
and enter the chamber where love really occurs,
does this image endure? Or is fire quenched by earth?

---

Fire and earth feed each other.
The earth provides fuel. The fire provides warmth.
