*The Stag Returns
12 June 2026

The stag returns to the woods
alert to its many changing leaves,
the multicolored epiphanies of those
who are lost, now found, now lost again.
It will be a cool afternoon here
under the shade of these living pillars, 
testaments to life itself. But don't tarry,
he says, do not linger here under
the false impression that you might belong
at last in this forest. It is no home,
it offers no rest. A voice echoes
which once had been silent, and he sees
his purpose served. He moves on,
after pausing to soak the sound in.
