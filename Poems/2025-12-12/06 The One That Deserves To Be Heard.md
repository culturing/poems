*The One That Deserves To Be Heard
12 December 2025

<figure>
    <blockquote>
        <div>Never again would birds' song be the same.</div>
        <div>And to do that to birds was why she came.</div>
    </blockquote>
    <figcaption>Robert Frost, <cite>Never Again Would Birds' Song Be The Same</cite></figcaption>
</figure>

That change, do you feel it? Now your voice persisting
has made this bird sing a brighter tune, in harmony
with yours, and with the deeper well of things
from which you've come, to roost among birds, a bird yourself,
the most beautiful bird, the one that deserves to be heard.
