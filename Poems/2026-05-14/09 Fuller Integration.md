Fuller Integration
14 May 2026

<figure>
    <blockquote>
        Man is in love and loves what vanishes,</br>
        What more is there to say? 
    </blockquote>
    <figcaption>Yeats, <cite>Nineteen-Hundred and Nineteen</cite></figcaption>
</figure>

They cannot last, these dreams
of love come down to roost
on earth from heaven. They may
point the way toward a fuller integration
of our many selves, a deeper understanding,
but they are no destination.
