Woman Now
10 March 2026

Are you a woman now? I don't know
if I ought to hope, but I'm certainly *curious*.
Something was different. You have come a long way.

--

But some things have not changed at all,
and perhaps will not change for a long time.
I would like to know you. What do you fear?

--

But your fear has diminished. You name your desires now
without hesitation. You show them with courage,
although they involve much sorrow.

--

But showing is not quite action.
I doubt that more showing will convince me of anything.
I know you are very *aware*. What can you *do*?
