*Sophia Again
02 March 2026

Sophia, long have I searched in vain
for your hollow song, your dying fall, your wisdom
echoing through life and not in the mind alone.
But I will not give up. I *cannot* give up, for your song
is true, and I sing this to you in constant resonation.
There are green fields somewhere, at least in the mind,
and if in the mind, then at least *in potentia* vivified
in splendors of sense. There are ways to you,
this much is true, and I'll find them, for I
will not rest until I do.
