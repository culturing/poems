*Theseus Says Goodbye
23 March 2026

Ariadne, it seems that you truly deserve your namesake.
Priestess of Dionysus you are and must remain.
I bow to your destiny. I will believe that you helped me
on purpose, in spite of the pain. It's better that way.

Now I must confess Dionysus divine.
But I serve gods who are more kind.

Think of me when you visit Athens.
