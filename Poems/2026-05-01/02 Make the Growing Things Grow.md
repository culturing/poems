Make the Growing Things Grow
01 May 2026

I cannot escape these mysteries, the deeper truths
of the unfolding universe, endless vibrations, the push
and pull of forgotten stars...we are all that we are,
and yet I find myself drawn skyward, toward
unknown secrets, awaiting investigation...

---

But out there are no roots, no nourishment
to strengthen bodies, to tie us down to each other,
in place and growing, forever growing, becoming
our stronger selves...

---

Is there some combination that synthesizes
mind and body? The wisdom of stars
brought down to earth, to make the growing things grow?
